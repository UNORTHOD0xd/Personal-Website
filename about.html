About Me

I’m Dorien Bourne, a cybersecurity researcher and computer scientist passionate about securing digital ecosystems. With a strong foundation in cryptography, ethical hacking, and smart contract security, I explore vulnerabilities in systems to build stronger, more resilient security solutions.

My journey in cybersecurity is fueled by a deep curiosity for how technology can be both a tool for innovation and a target for exploitation. I specialize in analyzing and securing blockchain-based applications, conducting smart contract audits, and developing security-focused solutions to mitigate threats in decentralized systems.

Beyond blockchain security, I have a keen interest in network security, penetration testing, and zero-knowledge proofs. I actively research cutting-edge security paradigms and contribute to the cybersecurity community through hands-on projects and continuous learning.

When I’m not deep-diving into security research, you’ll find me refining my coding skills, experimenting with new cryptographic techniques, or working on my latest project—pushing the boundaries of secure digital interactions.
